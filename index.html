Before you shoot
How to position
yourself
So it looks like you're standing together
01
Stand against a plain background
A solid wall. No windows, no shelves.
02
Stand an arm's length from your camera
Waist up should be visible. Not just your face. Your body language is part of the photo.
03
Center yourself. Same height as them.
Head at the same level as your partner's. Equal space around you in frame.
04
Good light, on your face
Face a window or lamp. Not behind you.
